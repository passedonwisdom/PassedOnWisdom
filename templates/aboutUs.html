About Us

Passed On Wisdom is a Campus Company Based in K. J. Somaiya College of Engineering Run by 4 Students from Third Year IT Engineering. We at Passed On Wisdom Aim to Provide the Younger Generations a Marketplace to Buy All the Necessary Items for a College Starter Pack at a Cheaper Price. Passed On Wisdom is a Platform for All to Post Ads for and Purchase these Items as well as Buy and Sell Reference Books. This Idea is Driven by all Four of us having a Fire within ourselves to Experience Business First hand in College itself! The Idea was seeded in the Early 2020 but was Delayed due to te COVID-19 Pandemic. After Endless Hours of Brainstorming, HardWork, Team Meets and Discussions, Passed On Wisdom is Finally Live! Do use this service and tell your Friends too!

In Case of Any Complaints, Queries, Suggestions or Anything Else, Feel Free to Contact Any of Us and We Will Be More than Happy to Assist You!
